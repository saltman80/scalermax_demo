Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2025

1. Introduction

ScalerMax FE is a static, demo frontend designed to showcase the ScalerMax AI Intent Server. We prioritize your privacy and do not collect or store personal information through this demo application.

2. Information We Collect

No personal data is collected or stored. Any input you enter (e.g., chat messages) is sent directly to the ScalerMax AI API for real-time processing and not retained on our servers.

3. Use of Data

All user inputs are used solely to provide interactive AI chat functionality during your session. Once the session ends or the browser is closed, no data persists.

4. Cookies, Tracking, and Local Storage

This demo does not use cookies, web beacons, or other tracking technologies. We do not embed analytics or third-party trackers on this site. However, we use localStorage to persist your theme preference (light or dark mode) across sessions.

5. Third-Party Services

We utilize the ScalerMax AI Intent Server API to process chat interactions. Please review the ScalerMax API Privacy Policy for details on their data handling practices.

6. Children's Privacy

This demo is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. The revised policy will include the effective date.

8. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@scalermax.ai.
